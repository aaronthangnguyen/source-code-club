# PROPOSAL #1: Discord Roles and Titles

<br>

## Roles

4 roles:

- Professor or Advisor
- Officer
- Staff
- Member  

<br>

## Titles

8 titles:

- Professor or Advisor
- President
- Vice President
- Treasurer
- Secretary
- Director
- Dev Team
- Member (implicit)

<br>

## Instruction
